Hanson Alister

Fan-wiki page

Appearance:

Han “Cuffs” Alister is a tall, blonde, sixteen year old considered to be very handsome. His eyes are a bright/brilliant blue and his hair is a golden blonde. He has high cheekbones, a long/straight nose, lean/muscular frame, and his smile is alluring.

He makes his first appearance in the first chapter of the first book The Demon King and is one of the series’ protagonists.

Personality:

        Han is a more proactive, hypervigilant, and hyperactive person compared to his perpetually calm and level-headed friend Fire Dancer. This makes sense considering just a year before the story starts he was the leader of the gang in his neighborhood, the Raggers. Han also does well under pressure and although he appears to improvise a lot (and does and probably got good at it from his time on the streets), he does make plans. He just knows that “”[a].

Because of his experiences as leader of the Raggers, he transitions nearly seamlessly into the similarly cutthroat environment of blueblood politics. However, he does chafe at the two-faced nature of politics and even points out that “”[b].

Because of how perilous his life has been, Han yearns for structure and calm, but realizes throughout the series that nowhere is safe and that he has to enjoy and protect the things he loves himself.

Family & Background:

        Han has a mother, father, and younger sister that are related to him by blood. He also has Fire Dancer, Willo, and Lucious as his found family (brother, mother, and father/friend respectively). Unfortunately, his father died in a war just a couple years after he was born and his mother and sister Mari die in fire in the first book. Lucious also passes away in the first book after Han undoes the immortality spell Lucious placed on himself over a millenia ago.

        Han grew up poor in Ragmarket. To survive he joined and eventually led a gang called the Raggers, stealing, pickpocketing, and gambling to make money for their families. Han also had a lot of interact with the Spirit Clan. He’s fostered at Marisa Pines Camp every summer since he was seven years old and was even given the clan name Hunts Alone. Although he’d been with the Raggers for almost as long as he’d been fostered with the clan, those two parts of his lives never intersected until the inciting incident of the first book.

Clothing:

        Han wears a few outfits throughout the series. His usual wardrobe is a motley of plain and valuable clothing consisting of a worn plain woolen shirt and trousers[c] (which are often too tight because he’s outgrown them), and a clan-made deerskin jacket, moccasins[d], and knee-high soft-leather boots. When in the city, he chooses to forgo his more expensive clothes and wears his well-worn moccasins. When he is forced to lay low in Marisa Pines Camp, he wears traditional clan clothing: ……..[e] When he is studying at Mystwerk at Oden’s Ford, he wears the uniform robes. When he attends fancy or political events he wears a suit & tie[f] along with his wizard stoles. Han almost always wears his talismans, amulet, and concealed knives in the series.

Fire Dancer[g]

fan-wiki page

Appearance:

        Fire Dancer (aka Hayden Bayer) has an angular bone structure and heavy dark brows, startlingly blue eyes, and a tawny bronzed skin tone. He is slender and fine-boned. He is slightly shorter than Han, but taller than Cat Tyburn.

        He makes his first appearance in the first chapter of the first book The Demon King.

Personality:

        Dancer is typically a calm and reserved person. He would usually rather avoid problems and conflicts and live his life, unless he considers it a serious transgression.

        Fire Dancer always has Han’s back and vice versa.

Because of the history surrounding his birth, Dancer has a deep, personal hatred for wizards, particularly the Bayers. He also has self-hatred for being a wizard himself and the result of the rape of his mother, but he gradually overcomes.

Family & Background:

        Fire Dancer lives with his mother Willo Watersong and extended family at Marisa Pines Camp in the Spirit Mountains. He rarely leaves camp and is not very sociable there. His “father” is High Wizard Gaven Bayer, who broke the Naeming and trespassed on clan territory (the Spirit Mountains) to rape Willo. His other “family” are Micah Bayer (younger brother), Fiona Bayer (younger sister), and Arkeda & Miphis Mander (cousins).

        Dancer’s closest — and likely only — friends are his cousin Digging Bird (later named Night Bird) and Han.

        He is the heir of Marisa Pines Camp since his mother is the matriarch.

Clothing:

Dancer almost always wears clan clothing; it’s what he grew up with and feels comfortable in. Even at Oden’s Ford, when he didn’t have to wear the uniform, he wore clan clothing and considered Valefolks’ clothes to be too tight and uncomfortable. He also wears his hair in beaded braids.[h] 

Micah Bayer

fan-wiki page

Appearance:

        Micah is pale-faced with straight, black, shoulder-length hair and a widow’s peak.

Personality:

        Micah is arrogant and elitist, more often than not

Family & Background:

        Micah grew up in a rich, politically and magically powerful family. His father, Gaven Bayer, is the High Wizard, and his mother comes from a family of almost equally powerful wizards. His twin sister is Fiona, and his cousins are Miphis and Arkeda Mander. His half-brother is Fire Dancer.

Clothing:

        Micah always wears fancy clothing along with his wizard stoles and multiple rings on his long fingers.
